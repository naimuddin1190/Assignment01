Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Fostering a culture of creativity and technological advancement to drive economic growth and improve efficiency.

Sustainability

Sustainability in Bangladesh focuses on balancing economic growth with environmental protection and social equity. It aims to ensure that current development meets the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs

Infrastructure

Developing robust and resilient infrastructure, including transportation, energy, telecommunications, and water and sanitation, to support economic activity and social progress.

Empowerment

Investing in human capital through education, healthcare, and women's empowerment to create a skilled and engaged workforce capable of driving sustainable development.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital
transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Charity becomes a way to shrug off our responsibility. But charity is no solution to poverty. Charity only perpetuates poverty by taking the initiative away from the poor. Charity allows us to go ahead with our own lives without worrying about the lives of the poor. Charity appeases our consciences.”

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
